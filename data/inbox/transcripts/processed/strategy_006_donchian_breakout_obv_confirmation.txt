STRATEGY: Donchian Channel Breakout with OBV Volume Confirmation
ASSET: BTC, ETH
TIMEFRAME: 4h
MARKET CONDITION: Breakout/expansion after consolidation
TYPE: Breakout/momentum
CONCEPT: Classic turtle trading breakout adapted for crypto. Price breaking the 20-period Donchian high/low signals a potential new trend. OBV confirmation ensures the breakout has real volume behind it, filtering fake breakouts. ADX rising confirms momentum is building. Exit uses a shorter 10-period Donchian to capture the meat of the move without giving back too much.
INDICATORS USED:
- Donchian Channels (period 20) - breakout entry signal
- Donchian Channels (period 10) - exit signal (shorter period)
- OBV - volume confirmation (OBV must be above its own 20-period SMA)
- ADX (period 14) - rising ADX confirms building momentum
- ATR (period 14) - position sizing and initial stop
ENTRY LONG:
1. Price closes above 20-period Donchian Channel high (new 20-bar high)
2. OBV > SMA(OBV, 20) (volume is supporting the move)
3. ADX(14) > 20 (some trend strength exists)
4. ADX is rising (current ADX > ADX from 3 bars ago)
ENTRY SHORT:
1. Price closes below 20-period Donchian Channel low (new 20-bar low)
2. OBV < SMA(OBV, 20) (volume confirms selling)
3. ADX(14) > 20
4. ADX is rising
EXIT RULES:
- Primary exit long: Price touches 10-period Donchian low (shorter channel catches trend end faster)
- Primary exit short: Price touches 10-period Donchian high
- Initial stop loss: 2.0x ATR(14) from entry
- No take profit target (let winners run, Donchian exit handles it)
- Max hold time: 50 candles (200 hours on 4h)
RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Risk per trade: 1% of account
- Max concurrent positions: 1 per asset
- If breakout candle is larger than 3x ATR, skip (overextended entry, poor R:R)
- Position size based on distance to initial stop
RATIONALE:
Donchian breakouts capture the start of major moves. The problem with breakout strategies is false breakouts - OBV and ADX filters address this directly. Using a shorter exit channel (10 vs 20) is the key insight from turtle trading: you want a more sensitive exit to protect profits while keeping a wider entry to avoid noise. Crypto’s trending nature makes breakout strategies particularly effective.
FALSIFIABLE HYPOTHESIS:
BTC/ETH 4h breakouts above 20-period Donchian highs with OBV confirmation and rising ADX produce profit factor above 1.3, with winning trades capturing moves of 3+ ATR.
PARAMETER RANGES FOR SWEEP:
- Entry Donchian period: 15-30, step 5
- Exit Donchian period: 5-15, step 5
- OBV SMA period: 10-30, step 10
- ADX threshold: 15-25, step 5
- ADX lookback for rising: 2-5, step 1
- Stop ATR multiplier: 1.5-2.5, step 0.5
- Max entry candle size: 2.0-4.0 ATR, step 0.5
