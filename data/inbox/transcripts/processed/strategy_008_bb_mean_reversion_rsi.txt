STRATEGY: Bollinger Band Mean Reversion with RSI Confirmation
ASSET: BTC, ETH
TIMEFRAME: 1h
MARKET CONDITION: Ranging/mean-reverting with established trend context
TYPE: Mean reversion within trend
CONCEPT: Trade bounces off Bollinger Band extremes but ONLY in the direction of the larger trend. This prevents counter-trend mean reversion trades that get steamrolled. RSI confirms the oversold/overbought condition is genuine. Price targeting the middle band (SMA 20) gives a clear mechanical exit.
INDICATORS USED:
- Bollinger Bands (period 20, std dev 2.0) - identify price extremes
- RSI (period 14) - confirm oversold/overbought
- EMA 200 - major trend filter (only trade with trend)
- ATR (period 14) - stop loss sizing
- SMA 20 - take profit target (Bollinger middle band)
ENTRY LONG:
1. Price closes at or below lower Bollinger Band
2. RSI(14) < 30 (oversold confirmation)
3. Price is above EMA 200 (we are in an uptrend, this is a pullback)
4. Previous candle was also below or touching lower BB (sustained touch, not spike)
ENTRY SHORT:
1. Price closes at or above upper Bollinger Band
2. RSI(14) > 70 (overbought confirmation)
3. Price is below EMA 200 (we are in a downtrend, this is a bounce)
4. Previous candle was also above or touching upper BB
EXIT RULES:
- Take profit: Price reaches SMA 20 (middle Bollinger Band)
- Stop loss: 2.0x ATR(14) beyond the Bollinger Band that triggered entry
- Time stop: If middle band not reached within 12 candles (12 hours), exit at market
- Breakeven: Move stop to entry once price moves 1.0 ATR in favor
RISK MANAGEMENT:
- Risk per trade: 0.75% of account (smaller size for mean reversion)
- Max concurrent positions: 1 per asset
- Skip entry if Bollinger Band width is expanding rapidly (bands widening = breakout, not mean reversion)
- Skip if ADX > 30 (strong trend makes mean reversion dangerous)
RATIONALE:
Bollinger Band touches in the direction of the major trend are high-probability setups because the trend acts as a magnet pulling price back. RSI confirmation filters out the touches that happen during momentum (which would continue through the band). The middle band target is conservative but consistent.
FALSIFIABLE HYPOTHESIS:
BTC/ETH 1h pullbacks to lower Bollinger Band during uptrends (price > EMA 200) with RSI < 30 revert to the middle band within 12 candles at least 55% of the time, producing profit factor above 1.15.
PARAMETER RANGES FOR SWEEP:
- BB period: 15-25, step 5
- BB std dev: 1.5-2.5, step 0.25
- RSI threshold long: 25-35, step 5
- RSI threshold short: 65-75, step 5
- EMA trend filter: 100-200, step 50
- Stop ATR multiplier: 1.5-2.5, step 0.5
- Time stop candles: 8-16, step 4
