TradingView indicator: RSI Extension + Divergence by TheRealDrip2Rip. RSI Div+ combines RSI overbought/oversold context, regular + hidden divergence signals, and a trend “ribbon” filter into one clean overlay. Divergences are drawn directly on price: Bullish Divergence (price makes a lower low while RSI makes a higher low) Bearish Divergence (price makes a higher high while RSI makes a lower high) Hidden Bullish / Hidden Bearish divergences (trend-continuation variants) Pivot dots help visualize the swing points used to confirm divergence.
