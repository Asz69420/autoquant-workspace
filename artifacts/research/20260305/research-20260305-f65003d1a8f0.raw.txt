TradingView indicator: Future Roll Monitor by QuantNomad. Rolling futures is not just a mechanical action — it is a structural decision. When you move from one contract to the next, you are exposed to changes in price, liquidity, and term structure. The difference between contracts (the “roll”) can quietly add cost, generate yield, or signal shifts in supply and demand dynamics. Future Roll Monitor helps you understand what is happening beneath the surface during contract transitions.
