Five independent frameworks from different traditions all identify regime transition detection as a general market edge mechanism. This convergence elevates the transition-detection thesis from validated hypothesis to near-established principle.

1. **Vortex Indicator** (technical analysis) — VALIDATED. Vortex v3a PF=2.034, all-regime. Detects directional momentum shifts via positive/negative vortex crossover.

2. **Ichimoku TK Cross** (Japanese charting) — VALIDATED. Ichimoku TK v1 PF=1.604, all-regime. Detects median-price transitions via Tenkan-Kijun crossover. Different math, same edge.

3. **Smart Money Concepts CHoCH/BOS** (institutional trading) — CONCEPTUAL (0.70 confidence). CHoCH = character change (maps to Vortex cross). BOS = break of structure (maps to Ichimoku TK cross). U32 alignment, reinforced by GuarDeer and Smart Pivot research cards in U34.

4. **VIX-VIXEQ Regime Detector** (volatility analysis) — NEW U34 RESEARCH CARD. Compares realized vs expected volatility to detect regime shifts BEFORE price moves. We have ATR (realized vol) but would need implied volatility proxy. Source: HenriqueCentieiro on TradingView.

5. **Euphoria Indicator** (behavioral finance) — NEW U34 RESEARCH CARD. Identifies exhaustion via emotion-mapped momentum extremes (-100 to +100). Detects when momentum psychology shifts from greed to fear or vice versa. Maps to CCI/RSI extreme zones + momentum divergence. Source: R2D2_4Life on TradingView.

Meta-observation: every all-regime ACCEPT in our portfolio uses some form of regime-adaptive or transition-detecting mechanism. Pure trend-following and pure mean-reversion both fail — adaptivity to regime change IS the edge.
