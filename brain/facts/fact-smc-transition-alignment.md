Smart Money Concepts from institutional trading use structural regime-transition signals that map directly to our proven transition-detection indicators:

- **CHoCH (Change of Character):** Detects when price character changes from trending to ranging or vice versa. Structurally equivalent to Vortex crossover (VTXP crosses VTXM) — both detect the MOMENT a trend's character shifts.
- **BOS (Break of Structure):** Detects structural level breaks in price direction. Maps to Ichimoku TK cross (ITS_9 crosses IKS_26) — both detect when price decisively breaks prior structure.

This provides **independent conceptual validation** of transition-detection as a general market mechanism from a completely different analytical tradition (institutional/Smart Money vs technical indicators). Three independent frameworks now identify the same edge:

1. Vortex crossover (momentum math)
2. Ichimoku TK cross (median-price math)
3. SMC CHoCH/BOS (price structure analysis)

**Confidence 0.70** — conceptual alignment only. No backtested evidence. Requires CHoCH/BOS indicators to be implemented and tested before confidence can increase.

**Implication:** If CHoCH/BOS can be encoded as dataframe columns, they would be a third independent transition-detection implementation candidate. Order Block and Fair Value Gap concepts from same SMC framework may provide complementary entry/exit logic.
