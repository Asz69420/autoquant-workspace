The transitional regime (between trending and ranging) consistently produces the highest single-regime PF across strategies. Vortex strategies specialise here due to transition-detection mechanics.

- Vortex v3a transitional PF=3.886 (highest ever)
- Vortex v2c transitional PF=2.986
- Vortex v3b transitional PF=2.250
- CCI Chop Fade transitional PF=1.521
- Non-vortex strategies tend to underperform in transitional (MACD 12:1 trans PF=0.569)
