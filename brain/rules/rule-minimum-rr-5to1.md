All unique ACCEPT-tier strategies use 5:1 or higher R:R. Lower R:R ratios (2:1, 3:1) have 0 ACCEPTs. The win rate on most strategies is 17-25%, requiring high R:R to achieve positive expectancy. 8:1 is the most successful ratio (KAMA Stoch v1, RSI pullback, Supertrend 8:1). 12:1 validated on RSI slingshot.
